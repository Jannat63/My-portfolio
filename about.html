My Story

I don't just optimise websites — I engineer digital visibility across every layer of modern search.

My journey started with a Bachelor's in Computer Science & Engineering at NUBT, Khulna. While most of my peers went into software development, I became fascinated by one question: why do some websites get found and others don't?

That curiosity grew into a career. Over three years I've worked as an IT Officer, Lead Web Strategist, and digital growth consultant — learning that visibility requires more than keywords. It requires understanding how Google, AI assistants, and Large Language Models think.

Today I specialise in the full search trinity: SEO (Google rankings), AEO (AI answers & featured snippets), and GEO (being cited by ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity). My Computer Science background means I can go deep on technical issues that most SEOs can't touch.
