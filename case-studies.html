E-Commerce Website SEO Campaign

An e-commerce client in Bangladesh came with near-zero organic presence. In 90 days, structured technical fixes, content optimization and strategic keyword targeting drove a 230% increase in organic traffic.

The Problem

  • Poor technical health — 400+ crawl errors, slow load times
  • No keyword strategy — all traffic from branded searches
  • Thin product page content, no schema markup

The Strategy

  • Full Screaming Frog crawl — fixed all 4xx errors and redirect chains
  • Core Web Vitals optimization — LCP from 6.2s to 2.1s
  • Product & category page keyword mapping & rewrite
  • Product schema, breadcrumb schema, and FAQ schema added
  • Internal linking restructure across 200+ pages

Results (90 Days)

  • +230% organic traffic (GSC verified)
  • 180+ new keywords in top 10
  • Average position improved from 42 to 11
  • Core Web Vitals: all green

Local Business — Google Map Pack #1

A Dhaka-based local service business had no Google Business Profile presence and was invisible for local searches. After a focused 60-day local SEO campaign, they ranked #1 in the Map Pack for their core keywords.

The Problem

  • No optimized Google Business Profile
  • Inconsistent NAP citations across the web
  • No reviews strategy — only 3 reviews total

The Strategy

  • Full Google Business Profile optimization with photos, services, posts
  • NAP consistency audit and citation cleanup across 40+ directories
  • Systematic review generation strategy
  • Local keyword targeting on website landing pages
  • LocalBusiness schema with geo-coordinates

Results (60 Days)

  • Ranked #1 in Google Map Pack for 5 core keywords
  • +150% local lead enquiries
  • Reviews grew from 3 to 47 (4.8★ average)
  • GBP profile views +320%

Blog & News Website — AEO Optimization

A news and blog website wanted visibility beyond page rankings. By restructuring existing content and adding conversational Q&A formats, the site captured 8 featured snippets and appeared in multiple People Also Ask boxes.

The Problem

  • Good content but zero featured snippet presence
  • No FAQ schema or structured Q&A format
  • Content not aligned to conversational search queries

The Strategy

  • Featured snippet opportunity analysis using Ahrefs
  • Rewrote 20 articles with direct-answer paragraph format
  • Added FAQ sections with schema to every post
  • Mapped PAA questions for each topic cluster
  • Added HowTo & Q&A schema where appropriate

Results (45 Days)

  • 8 featured snippet positions captured
  • +120% overall search visibility
  • Appeared in PAA boxes for 30+ queries
  • Click-through rate improved by +45%

Fashion E-Commerce — Meta Ads 4.2x ROAS

A fashion e-commerce brand was spending on Meta Ads with a 1.1x ROAS — barely breaking even. After a full campaign restructure, creative testing and a proper retargeting funnel, ROAS reached 4.2x in 60 days and revenue from paid social increased 180%.

The Problem

  • Single campaign targeting everyone — no audience segmentation
  • Zero retargeting — losing warm traffic that had visited the site
  • One creative format untested across all audiences — 1.1x ROAS

The Strategy

  • Rebuilt full campaign structure: Cold Awareness → Warm Engagement → Hot Conversion
  • Built Custom Audiences from website visitors, video viewers and past buyers
  • Created Lookalike Audiences from top 5% of customer purchase list
  • A/B tested 12 creative variants — video, carousel and single image hooks
  • Set up Conversions API alongside Pixel for accurate attribution

Results (60 Days)

  • 4.2x ROAS (up from 1.1x at the start)
  • 38% reduction in cost-per-purchase
  • Retargeting campaigns alone: 7.8x ROAS
  • Revenue from paid social +180%
